How to Kill Voldemort Within 3 Steps

First, to kill Voldemort, you have to show him respect. All the wizards and witches who didn’t show him respect was killed. For example: James and lily potter, the parents of Neville, and even Dumbledore. The list goes on. So, if you don’t want to be killed first, you have to show him some respect. However, I didn’t mean that you have to like him and go to the dark side. All I mean is that you have to look serious about fighting with him. You shouldn’t bring any personal emotions with you like harry. Who is the main character and nearly killed by one of the death eater. (in the deathly hollows) So, do not bring personal emotions!

Step 2. Use the expilliarmas charms BEFORE Voldemort could ever notice. Now, congratulations, you have succeed a half of the process. You get Voldemort’s wand now, laugh at him, and make he feels humiliated. Voldemort will definitely be furious and be irrational. So, at that point here are actually two situations. The first situation is that Voldemort takes his wand back. And he tries to use the 3 illegal curse to you. Don’t WORRY! True warriors are never afraid of the dark side. However, I believe now you have to take some actions. Think about all the charms and curses that you know. Pick one that you think is the most powerful. Remember, don’t hesitate. Think of the happy things that could make you feel strong and happy enough for you to be strong. As if you are going to produce a Patronus charm. That way, Voldemort might be badly hurt and you success. Second situation, the wands are actually all in your hand and Voldemort couldn’t take it. To be honest, this situation is a lot easier than the last one, you only have to shot the Avada Kedavra charm and kill Voldemort.

Step 3 now, you have beaten Voldemort and you have to go back to the place that you belong—–Hogwarts. But how? It is easy. Find a hippogriff that belong to Hagrid. Like Buckbeak. Than you can fly back to school. However, if you can’t find one, you can apparated. Easy?

Now, you have achieved a goal! You beat Voldemort and enjoy your life in the magic word!
